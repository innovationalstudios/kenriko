Ceremony details

  • General Event Time Line

    Ceremony will begin promptly at 6pm, followed by a cocktail "hour". Guests will be asked to move to the reception area for dinner and toasts. Dancing will begin and last until 11pm, when the groom and the bride will make their (grand) exit!

  • Dress Code

    Dress Code is Formal, but please be prepared for the summer blaze! Our ceremony and cocktail hour will be on a grassy, tree-covered area so please be mindful of footwear. The reception will be held half outdoors, half indoors with air conditioning, so ladies may prefer to have something to keep themselves warm, just in case. Please also be prepared for some dancing!

  • Accommodation

    For those from out of town who are interested in finding their own accomodation, we suggest searching in the following areas: Seattle, Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland, Issaquah. There is a variety of hostels, airbnbs, and hotels available.

  • Transportation

    Please plan an additional 15-30 minutes to your travel time; Greater Seattle traffic can be quite horrific during rush hour! For those without a car, there is one bus route that will stop 10minutes walking from Salish Lodge - Route 208 from Issaquah. In addition to local taxis, we suggest using Lyft or Uber for the most cost effective transport from Seattle to Salish Lodge.
